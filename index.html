Golden Mashed Potatoes and Beef Square Gravy

Description

Ingredients Potatoes 1-1/2 pound Yukon gold potatoes 1/2 stick butter 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce 1 cup powdered milk As needed water to boil potatoes 1 teaspoon salt Meat squares 1-1/2 pound ground beef 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper 2 tablespoons peanut oil to fry squares Beef scramble 1/2 pound ground beef 2 large leeks 1 cup celery sliced 2 tablespoons dehydrated onions 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon ground white pepper 1/2 cup all purpose flour

Cooking

1

Wash the leeks and celery very well. I am not exaggerating to say I washed out a 1/2 cup of sand. Slice the leeks and celery. Set aside. Cube the potatoes cover with water add the salt. Boil till fork tender. Pour out all but a cup of the water. Add 1/2 stick of butter, Worcestershire sauce, and powdered milk.

2

Stir and mash the potatoes if more water is needed add a bit at a time. Press the ground beef flat. Adding salt and pepper. Heat the oil. Take the 1/2 pound of ground beef for scrambling add to the pan you fried the little squares. Add the leeks, garlic and celery.

3

Sauté till leeks and celery are tender. When tender add the dehydrated onions. Stir in the onions and then add the tablespoon of butter. Add potatoes in a bowl form a crater and fill with the gravy beef squares. Serve I hope you!!!
